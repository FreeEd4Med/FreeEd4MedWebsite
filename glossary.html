Glossary

Assuagement

Assuagement — the act of making something burdensome or painful less intense or severe, such as grief or pain.

Usage: We use the term to describe the clinical and emotional goals of music interventions — practices and tracks that reduce the intensity of experiences like anxiety or pain.
