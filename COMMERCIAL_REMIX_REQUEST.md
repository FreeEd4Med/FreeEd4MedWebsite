Commercial remix / release request
--------------------------------

Thank you for your interest in commercially releasing a remix of material from
FreeEd4Med's repository. The content here (stems, masters, or sections of tracks)
is licensed under CC BY‑NC‑SA 4.0 by default and **does not allow** commercial
use without an explicit license.

If you would like permission for a commercial release, please provide the
following information via email to freeed4med@gmail.com and attach this form as a
starting point. We will evaluate requests and may require a formal license and/or
revenue split.

Required information:
- Your name / group / label
- Contact email
- Which track and which assets or stems you want to use (paths/names)
- Intended distribution: stores/services (Spotify, Apple, YouTube, Bandcamp, etc.)
- Proposed master ownership and/or percentage split (e.g., master split 50/50)
- Proposed composition writing split (how will songwriting royalties be shared?)
- Proposed launch date and distributor (if known)
- Any other collaborators or sample sources used

Minimum licensing terms (example, negotiable):
- Master revenue split: 50% to FreeEd4Med, 50% to remixer (negotiable)
- Songwriting split: FreeEd4Med credited with at least 50% of composition (register with PRO)
- All commercial releases must include credits and metadata listing FreeEd4Med as a copyright holder
- Remixer must register splits with distributor and PROs before release and supply ISRC/UPC and registration proof

Note: This is a template — details will be agreed in writing. We reserve the right
to deny any request.
