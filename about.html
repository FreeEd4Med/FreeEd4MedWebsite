About FreeEd4Med

FreeEd4Med.org blends evidence‑based medical education with creative music partnerships. We work with artists (including MadMoozeMusic) and partners like Songs of Love Foundation to create music‑for‑healing resources, clinical programs, and community outreach.

We use the term assuagement (see Glossary) to describe reducing the intensity of experiences such as pain, anxiety, and grief — a central goal of music-for-healing work.
